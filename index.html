Venice Beach

The Venice Skatepark is an amazing place to skate and the perfect setting for AWOL. The park is tucked between the ocean and the boardwalk in the iconic town of Venice Beach. It's where the modern skateboarding was born, and where it will be reborn in video games. From the way the morning sun illuminates the famous VENICE sign to the paint your skateboards leaves on a rail, we will sweat every detail to immerse you in Venice Beach.
